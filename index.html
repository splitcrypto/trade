About FreeMoneyCoin (FREE)

Quick summary: Vault mints tokens at a vault price (1 POL by default). Secondary buys must pay between public min and max prices; owners can configure parameters. Sellers can sell limited amounts back to the vault for a preset buyback price. Decimals are 0 (whole coins).

  • Buy from vault (mint) at vault price.
  • Buy from holders inside min/max range; buyer obtains bonus minted tokens.
  • Sellers can sell back up to half of their vault-acquired tokens (vault allowance).
  • Owner-only controls exist for pause, withdraw, params.
